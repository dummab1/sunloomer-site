What Sunloomer does

A specialist partner for the integrations standard drivers leave behind.

Every luxury home has a few systems that the catalog driver doesn’t cover — a rare boat lift, a custom shade controller, a niche sauna or e-shower. Sunloomer designs and develops the drivers that bring those systems cleanly into Control4.

Custom Device Drivers

Bespoke Control4 drivers for IP, serial, and protocol-bridged devices — engineered to behave like first-party integrations.

02

IP & Serial Integration

About Sunloomer

A specialized integration studio, not a smart-home brand.

Sunloomer was founded by a longtime Control4 homeowner and tech enthusiast who was frustrated that drivers for many unique home automation devices were unavailable.

The studio focuses on practical, well-built drivers that extend the value of Control4 in real homes — built carefully, supported quietly, and designed to feel like they were always part of the system.
